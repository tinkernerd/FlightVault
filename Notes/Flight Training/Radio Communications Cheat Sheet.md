## TOWER
[https://www.liveatc.net/search/?icao=KSEE](http://www.liveatc.net/search/?icao=KSEE)
### Take Off
Pilot - Gillespie Tower, Cessna 734NK holding short 27 Right, Right down wind departure.

Tower - Cessna 734NK, Gillespie Tower, Runway 27 Right, cleared for take oﬀ, right down wind departure approved.

Pilot - 27 Right, Cleared for take oﬀ, right down wind departure, Cessna 4NK.

-OR-

Tower - Cessna 734NK, Gillespie Tower, Hold Short, 27 Right.

Pilot - **Hold Short 27 Right - Cessna 734NK (READ BACK WITH FULL CALL SIGN!)**

### Landing

Pilot - Gillespie Tower, Archer 5676V, three thousand five hundred, Barona Casino, with Delta.

Tower - Archer 5676V, Gillespie Tower, make right base 27 Right, report Lakeside Golf Course (or Lake Jennings).

Pilot - Right base 27 Right, report Lakeside Golf Course, Archer 76V. Pilot - Archer 76V Lakeside golf course.

Tower - Archer 76V, following Bonanza 3 mile final, number 3, 27 right, cleared to land. Pilot - Number 3, 27 right, cleared to land, Archer 76V.

### Class Delta Transition

Pilot - Ramona Tower, Archer 5676V, 3500, Barona Casino, Northbound Transition.

Ramona Tower - Archer 5676V, Ramona Tower, Northbound transition approved at or above 3500, Ramona altimeter 30.05.

Pilot - Northbound transition approved at or above 3500, 30.05, Archer 76V.
